## Codebase Patterns
- Student ID format: 10 digits (YYYYXXYYYY) where YYYY=year, XX=school code, XXXX=student number
- Alternative student ID: 6-12 alphanumeric characters for continuing education
- Use `sql<number>` template for Prisma queries
- Always use `IF NOT EXISTS` for migrations
- Export types from auth.ts for UI components
